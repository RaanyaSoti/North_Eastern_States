All About- 'Arunachal Pradesh'

Arunachal Pradesh is the north-eastern most state of India.

It shares international borders with Bhutan in the west, Myanmar in the east, and a disputed border with China in the north at the McMahon Line.

The capital of Arunachal Pradesh is 'Itanagar'.

The population of Arunachal Pradesh as of 2012 is estimated to be 12.6 lakhs crores.
